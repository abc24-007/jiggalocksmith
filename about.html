Our Story

Founded in 2020, JIGGAlocksmiths began as a small local business with a mission to provide reliable, affordable locksmith services to the Mmopane community. What started as a one-person operation has grown into a trusted team of certified locksmiths serving residential and commercial clients throughout the Kweneng District.

Our founder, Christopher Mokokonyane, recognized the need for honest, professional locksmith services after experiencing poor service from other providers. He set out to create a company that would prioritize customer satisfaction, quick response times, and quality workmanship above all else.
